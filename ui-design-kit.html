About the Project

The IOS 13 UI design kit was a project I created to "Scratch my own itch". I needed some lockscreen elements for the new IOS udate but couldn't find them anywhere. As I started to make it I realized that other people would probably want want this too. So I expanded on what I was designing and created a complete design kit with battery states, notifications, and even gesture designs.

The problem

Creating all the elements for the IOS 13 lockscreen including use cases.

One of three columns

The Final Design

The final design can be seen in [FIGURE 21]. For this project the prototype was already built as IOS 13 had just lauched. So I grabbed my phone and interacted with it as much as I could and tansfered what I saw into usuable SKetch elements. Many of the elements had different states based on frequency, gestures, and location on the lockscreen. Overall, the project came out great with design elemsts for different sized devices.
